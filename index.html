I am an Associate Professor (IEEE/CCF Senior Member) at State Key Laboratory of Autonomous Intelligent Unmanned Systems, Tongji University. My research interest primarily centers on human-centric AI and affective computing. In this field, I co-organize a series of challenges and workshops (MER@IJCAI, MRAC@ACM Multimedia, MEIJU@ICASSP), establish benchmark (MERBench) and toolbox (MERTools), and propose new tasks to enhance accuracy and reliability (EMER, OV-MER, AffectGPT, AffectGPT-R1, EmoPrefer). I also serve as Associate Editor at IEEE TAFFC, IEEE TASLP and Pattern Recognition, Area Editor at Information Fusion, Area Chair at ACM Multimedia 2025 and ACL ARR 2025, ACM Multimedia 2026 Dataset Co-Chair. My short-term goal is to establish a new pathway toward more reliable and accurate emotion recognition techniques. My long-term goal is to enhance real-world human-AI interaction, ensuring robustness, trust, privacy, efficiency, and responsible deployment in areas such as healthcare, education, finance, etc.

I am recruiting visiting students, undergraduate, masters, and PhD in Tongji University.
